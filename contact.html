Meet the Team

We are researchers passionate about building the next generation of intelligent agents. Reach out — we'd love to hear from you.

OpenSage is a research project developing an AI-centric agent generation engine that lets models self-program agent topology, synthesize toolsets, and manage structured memory — no hand-crafting required. Our team spans AI security, software engineering, and systems research.
